Who Are We.

Ever since its establishment in 1937,the poor homes society has been taking care of the person aged above 55 who areorphaned,crippedor unable to earn their livelihood due to physical defects,infirmity or desease but having sufficient health to manage themselves,by giving them shelter,food,clothes and medical care.Thousands of such people have enjoyed these facilities ever since.

Founder.

The poor home society was founded by sri.k.n kurup.He hailed from an aristrocratic nair tharavadu near thirunavaaya.From 1934 to 1945,he worked as an accountant in a company under british management named madhura comopany,located in beach road,kozhikode.

Establishment

The poor home society was established in 1937.It was registerd and given permit by the orphonage control board under social welfare department. In the begining the society was governed by a team of prominent citizens of kozhikode viz.Smt.A.V.Kuttimalu Amma and Sri.Kozhippurath Madhava Menon.

Our Goal.

Our main goal is to provide secured shelter for old peoples.
